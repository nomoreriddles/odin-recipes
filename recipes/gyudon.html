Gyudon

A simple beef and rice dish that uses typical Japanese seasonings. An easy but tasty dinner option for when you're feeling lazy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • approx. 60g of thinly sliced beef
  • spring onion (as garnish)
  • half a brown onion
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • (optional) egg

Steps

  1. cut onion and cook on a medium heat until slightly browned
  2. add meat and sugar and cook until meat is no longer red
  3. add soy sauce, sake and mirin, reduce heat to low for 3-5 minutes
  4. if using egg, whisk it and then pour over onions, beef and sauce. cover frying pan with lid and cook for around 1 to 2 minutes
  5. put rice in bowl, add ingredients on top. garnish with green onion
