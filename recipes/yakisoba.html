Yakisoba

A classic Japanese stir fry dish. Commonly served with pork but other protein can be substituted.

Ingredients

  • Half an onion
  • Half a carrot
  • 2 or more cabbage leaves
  • 2 shiitake mushrooms
  • 65g of thinly sliced pork
  • 2 tbsp of yakisoba sauce
  • green onion and beni shoga for garnish
  • Yakisoba noodles or equivalent

Steps

  1. Cut ingredients
  2. Heat wok on medium high, add oil
  3. When smoke comes off, add meat, onions, carrot
  4. After two minutes or meat is no longer pink, add cabbage and mushroom
  5. Boil water in seperate saucepan and cook noodles
  6. Reduce heat on wok to medium, add cooked noodles and yakisoba sauce, mix together
  7. Serve with garnish
